Common Questions

You probably want to know...

How long does it take to set up?
Most builds are live within 5-7 business days. We start as soon as you send over your business info (services, pricing, FAQs). You'll see a draft within 48 hours, we tweak based on your feedback, then we install it on your site.
Do I need a website for this to work?
No. The AI assistant works on your existing website if you have one — but we can also host it on a standalone page we create for you, or link it from your Facebook page, Google Business Profile, or anywhere you want.
What if I'm not technical at all?
Perfect — that's who this is for. You don't touch code. You don't install anything. We handle every technical piece from setup to deployment. You just provide info about your business and we do the rest.
How is this different from a regular chatbot?
Regular chatbots have scripted answers and break the moment a customer asks something off-script. This is a real AI assistant — it understands natural conversation, learns from your business data, and handles questions you didn't pre-program. And it captures leads automatically.
What does the monthly fee cover?
Hosting, monitoring, conversation reports, and up to 4 hours of updates per month — adding new services, retraining on new data, tweaking the system as your business grows. Cancel anytime.
What if customers ask something the bot doesn't know?
The bot is trained to gracefully say "let me have the owner get back to you" and collect their contact info. You never lose a lead because of an unknown question — you just get the lead with context.
Can I see exactly what people are asking my bot?
Yes. Every conversation is logged. You can review them anytime, and we send a monthly report highlighting common questions, hot leads, and missed opportunities — so your business gets smarter every month.
What if I want to cancel?
No long-term contract. Cancel anytime with 30 days notice. We'll export your data and conversation history before we shut it off — it's yours.
